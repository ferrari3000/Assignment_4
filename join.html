Become A Member Today

The best way to experience all that the Columbus Museum of Art has to offer is by becoming a member. Membership enables the Museum to preserve and expand a quality collection of art and make it accessible to all. Support from members also helps to create and present important exhibitions for our community and to develop innovative ways for the more than 200,000 annual Museum visitors to interact with art.
Membership to the Columbus Museum of Art provides free admission, free parking, invitations to special preview events, discounts and other exclusive member benefits. Join or Renew today to be a part of all of the exciting things happening at CMA!
Please contact Lauren Emond, Member Relations Manager at 614.629.0344 or lauren.emond@cmaohio.org with questions.

Membership Levels & Benefits

All Members Enjoy

  • Free unlimited admission to the permanent collection and Center for Creativity
  • Free parking
  • Invitations to members-only special exhibition previews
  • 10 percent discount in Museum Store and Schokko Art Café
  • Free or discounted admission to programs, events, and special exhibitions
  • Bimonthly member magazine and monthly e-newsletter
  • Discounts from local cultural partners
  • Each level of membership includes all benefits of the previous level(s).
